Hannah Muthoni – Founder & CEO, Dhalia Events

Hannah Muthoni is a visionary entrepreneur and the driving force behind Dhalia Events, a premier event planning and décor company known for delivering unforgettable experiences. With a deep passion for creativity, elegance, and precision, she has transformed countless weddings, corporate functions, and private celebrations into stunning realities.

Her leadership has positioned Dhalia Events as a trusted name in event planning, offering bespoke solutions that turn every occasion into a masterpiece.

Under her guidance, Dhalia Events continues to set new standards in event excellence, ensuring that every client’s vision is not only met but exceeded. Whether it’s a grand wedding, a high-profile corporate summit, or an intimate celebration, Hannah’s commitment to quality, detail, and personalization shines through in every event.
